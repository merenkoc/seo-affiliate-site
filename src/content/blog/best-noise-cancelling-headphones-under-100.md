## What to Look For
When choosing the best noise cancelling headphones under $100, there are several factors to consider. Here are a few key buying criteria to keep in mind:
* Noise cancellation technology: Look for headphones with advanced noise cancellation features, such as hybrid noise-cancellation technology.
* Sound quality: Consider headphones with high-quality sound reproduction, including clear highs and deep bass.
* Comfort and build quality: Choose headphones with comfortable earcups and a durable build that can withstand regular use.
* Battery life: Consider headphones with long battery life, especially if you plan to use them for extended periods.
