## What to Look For
When buying a TV, consider the following factors:
* Picture quality: Look for a TV with good color accuracy, contrast, and brightness.
* HDR support: If you want to watch HDR content, look for a TV that supports HDR10, HDR10+, or Dolby Vision.
* Refresh rate: A higher refresh rate, such as 120Hz, can provide smoother motion and a more immersive viewing experience.
* Smart TV features: Consider a TV with a user-friendly interface and support for popular streaming services like Netflix and Amazon Prime Video.

## FAQ

Q: What is the difference between HDR10 and HDR10+?

Q: Can I use a TV with a lower refresh rate for gaming?
A: While a lower refresh rate may not provide the same level of smoothness as a higher refresh rate, it can still provide a good gaming experience, especially if you're playing games that don't require fast motion.
